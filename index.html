The Case Genie

A completely decentralized friend-to-friend information sharing application.

Genie is an independent project, not affilated with any organization. If you need help with Genie, please contact us at , or consult a wise friend. No other entity would be better equipped or more willing to help you than these two resources. If someone is being paid to help you, you are doing it wrong.

Genie is powerful software, meant to be used for good. Unfortunately, we cannot tell you what you should share using Genie. You must use your own best judgment to determine what you will share.

Dependencies

  • A Computer
  • An Internet Connection
  • One of a select few IP addresses
  • JavaSE version 6
